Lasagna Soup

Description

Lasagna Soup is a delightful twist on the classic Italian lasagna. It combines the flavors of lasagna with the comforting warmth of a soup. This soup is filled with tender pasta, rich tomato broth, Italian spices, ground beef, and plenty of melted cheese. It's a hearty and satisfying dish that's perfect for chilly days.

Ingredients

  • 8 lasagna noodles, broken into pieces
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add chopped onions and garlic and cook until the onions are softened.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  4. Add the broken lasagna noodles and simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.
  5. Serve the soup in bowls and top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Optionally, broil in the oven for a few minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  6. Enjoy your homemade Lasagna Soup!
