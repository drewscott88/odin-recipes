Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a classic and indulgent dessert that's loved by many. This creamy and rich dessert consists of a buttery graham cracker crust topped with a velvety cream cheese filling. It can be enjoyed plain or with various toppings, such as fruit compote or chocolate ganache. Cheesecake is a delightful treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press this mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to create the crust.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese, 1 1/4 cups sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Mix in the sour cream and flour until the batter is well combined.
  5. Pour the cream cheese filling over the graham cracker crust in the pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set and the edges are lightly golden. The center should still have a slight jiggle.
  7. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven for 1 hour with the door slightly ajar.
  8. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, or overnight, before serving.
  9. Optional: Top with your favorite fruit compote, chocolate ganache, or whipped cream before serving.
  10. Slice and enjoy your delicious Cheesecake!
